#freo4freedom

a project of Refugee Rights Action Network

Saturday November 2, North Fremantle Bowling Club.

2PM
music and art workshops, bar open, lawn bowls
6:30PM
music, comedy performances and speakers

Join us for an afternoon of social education and collaboration, leading into an evening of insightful comedy and inspiring music as we come together to build a creative local movement for social and political change.

We want to hear your ideas to advance refugee rights in Australia! Contact us at freo@rran.org today.

Funds raised at the event will go to the highest voted community project working towards an end to mandatory detention and an end to offshore processing of asylum seekers.
